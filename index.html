About COLLOQUIUM

Colloquium is MCET's premier technical fest that brings together innovation, learning and competitive spirit under one platform. It is a celebration of technology, creativity and young engineering minds shaping the future. Experience an atmosphere filled with ideas, challenges and breakthrough moments.

Tradition of Excellence

Colloquium is the flagship technical event of Mohandas College of Engineering and Technology, Trivandrum. It serves as a platform where students demonstrate their technical expertise, explore new-age technologies and engage in thought-provoking competitions that foster innovation and problem-solving.
